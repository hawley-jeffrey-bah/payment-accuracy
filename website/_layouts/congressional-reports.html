Agency-level Oversight Reports

When the Federal Government makes payments to individuals and businesses as program beneficiaries, grantees, or contractors, or on behalf of program beneficiaries, it must make every effort to confirm that the right recipient is receiving the right payment for the right reason at the right time. The purpose of this website is to highlight efforts by Federal Agencies to reduce improper payments by intensifying their efforts to eliminate payment error, waste, fraud, and abuse in the major programs they administer.

PaymentAccuracy.gov is intended to increase transparency and provide easy access to accurate data, as submitted by the reporting agencies. Several reports are available to highlight these efforts, and can be accessed by searching below.

Make a selection in all three fields. Then click "Generate Report" to view the report.
