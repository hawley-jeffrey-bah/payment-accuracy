Agencies & Programs

The Payment Integrity Information Act of 2019 requires an annual publication to fulfill multiple statutory reporting requirements for both Agencies and the Office of Management and Budget. The Agency and Program profiles below lets users explore and compare payment integrity data through interactive visualizations.

Agency-wide Payment Integrity Overview

Take a closer look at the data and how federal agencies are managing susceptible programs and reducing and recovering improper payments.

Data shown on this page outlines the total dollar amount spent by programs determined by Agencies to be susceptible to improper payments. Not all Programs an Agency administers are susceptible to improper payments and therefore the outlays listed below and throughout the site do not include all Federal spending.
